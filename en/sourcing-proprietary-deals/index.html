CF Group Global Ltd looks to invest in exceptional small businesses across a range of industry sectors, where we believe there is the potential for significant capital growth.

While we understand that smaller company investments can carry greater risk, we mitigate these risks by conducting detailed due diligence and careful negotiation, by supporting investee companies through difficulties and by helping to promote growth.

Historically the team at CF Group Global Ltd has been able to generate strong deal flow through an extensive personal network of proven deal introducers. In addition, the team also benefits from the connections of the CF Group Global Ltd Syndicate and of the Advisory Board.

We also welcome introductions through advisers or directly from businesses seeking funding. Our aim is to provide a rapid initial response and, if not appropriate for our funds, offer suggestions of alternative sources of funding
